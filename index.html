Introducing Preconcept.ai

Tired of toiling to make the data match the unshakeable conclusions that your boss already holds? Preconcept automatically explores the space of possible analyses to find the ones that best fit the anointed story.

Sketch the graphs and talking points you want to support and Preconcept.ai will work backwards to generate the evidence—including queries, charts, and talking points—to support them. It’s like hyperparameter tuning for confirmation bias!

Preconcept.ai: Get the right analysis faster, using your special definition of “Right.”

"In the age of information overload, Preconcept.ai stands out as a game-changer, guiding organizations towards the most persuasive data to support their narratives."

About Us

We are a dedicated team of data analysts, engineers, and storytellers who have come together with a shared vision: to transform the way data analysis is done by embracing organizational reality. We understand that every organization has its unique challenges and priorities, and we believe in the power compelling narratives to shape data and drive success.



Our mission
To make data analysis effortless and efficient, enabling you to focus on what matters: telling the right story.


Our values
We believe in the reinforcing power of data and the importance of presenting it in the most effective way.
